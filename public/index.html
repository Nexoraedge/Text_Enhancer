Gemini Text Enhancer

Thank you for installing the Gemini Text Enhancer Chrome extension!

Getting Started:

  1. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Enter your Gemini API key in the settings tab
  3. Navigate to any website with text input fields
  4. Type your rough text in any input field
  5. Press Ctrl+G to enhance your text

The extension will automatically detect the context of the webpage and enhance your text accordingly.

For more information and troubleshooting, click on the extension icon and go to the Help tab.
